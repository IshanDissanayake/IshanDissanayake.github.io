Research Interests

In today's fast-paced world, vehicular accidents due to driver drowsiness have emerged as a critical concern for road safety. Drowsiness, often caused by factors such as fatigue, sleep deprivation, and monotony, significantly impairs a driver's cognitive abilities and reaction times, leading to an increased risk of accidents. To mitigate this issue and enhance road safety, the development of an efficient drowsiness detection and alerting system is of paramount importance.
